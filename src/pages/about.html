About Shop Analyser

What is Shop Analyser?

Shop Analyser is an AI-powered tool for Ugandan retailers and wholesalers. It helps you manage products, track profits, handle taxes, and get smart recommendations to grow your business. Powered by Otic Foundation.

Features

  • AI product recommendations
  • Profit graphs and analytics
  • Tax management for Uganda
  • Accountability and expense tracking
  • Print-ready reports for URA

How It Works

After signing up and logging in, users can access their dashboard where they can input product details, view profit graphs, and receive AI-driven recommendations. The tool simulates a backend experience, providing users with a comprehensive view of their business performance.
